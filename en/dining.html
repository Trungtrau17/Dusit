Dining at Dusit
Le Palais Tu Hoa Ha Noi

Explore Our Dining

Discover an array of elegant venues offering diverse culinary delights, each designed to elevate your dining experience with refined luxury and exceptional taste.

SOI Restaurant

Soi brings authentic Thai cuisine to life with bold flavours, fresh ingredients, and traditional techniques. A vibrant setting makes it the ideal place to enjoy Thai classics with a modern twist.

Dusit Gourmet

A chic café and bakery offering fresh pastries, gourmet sandwiches, premium coffee, and artisanal teas. Ideal for quick bites or a relaxed afternoon tea.

Palais Lounge

Our Lobby Bar provides a relaxed, elegant space to enjoy afternoon tea or drinks – a perfect spot to unwind and savour the moment.

Vinci Restaurant and Rooftop Bar

Vinci delivers a refined dining experience with elegant Italian flavors and serene views, ideal for a relaxed lunch or intimate dinner.

Phở Lụa

Phở Lụa offers a unique twist on traditional Vietnamese cuisine, featuring delicate rice noodles and a variety of fresh ingredients, all served in a stylish setting.

VIP Dining

VIP Dining Room offers refined Asian flavors, elegant interiors, and a private ambiance for intimate gatherings.
